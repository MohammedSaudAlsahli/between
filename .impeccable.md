## Design Context

### Users
Saudi e-commerce merchants (Arabic-first, bilingual AR/EN) evaluating fulfillment partners. They're making business decisions — they need to feel confidence, speed, and trust. Mobile-first audience.

### Brand Personality
Bold, Fast, Trusted. The dark + electric yellow (#F1DF01) brutalist aesthetic is intentional. This is not a soft SaaS — it's an industrial logistics powerhouse that moves fast.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark (#000 base) with electric yellow accents. Industrial, brutalist, high-contrast.
- **Fonts**: Space Grotesk (EN) + Tajawal (AR). Space Grotesk's geometric sharpness reinforces "fast, precise." Tajawal is a proven Arabic web font.
- **Motion**: Three.js hero, GSAP scroll reveals, custom cursor — all intentional. Keep but refine.
- **Anti-references**: No generic SaaS (purple gradients). No overly corporate. No playful/startup-ish.
- **Direction**: Keep current direction, polish what's there.

### Design Principles
1. **Bold over safe** — Yellow on black is the brand. Never dilute it.
2. **Speed in every detail** — Animations, interactions, and copy all convey velocity.
3. **Trust through precision** — Zero visual slop. Every alignment, spacing, and transition must be intentional.
4. **Arabic-first** — RTL is the default, not an afterthought.
5. **No AI slop** — No side-stripe borders, no gradient text, no glassmorphism, no generic rounded-rect cards with drop shadows.
